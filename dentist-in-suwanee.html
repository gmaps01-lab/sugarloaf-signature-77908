What is the process for starting Invisalign at Sugarloaf Signature Dentistry?
First, you'll schedule a free Invisalign consultation at our Suwanee clinic. We'll take digital scans of your teeth and discuss your goals. Most patients visit us every 6-8 weeks for progress checks until treatment is complete.
