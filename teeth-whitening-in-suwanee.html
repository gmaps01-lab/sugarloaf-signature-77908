Frequently Asked Questions About Teeth Whitening

How much does professional teeth whitening cost at Sugarloaf Signature Dentistry in Suwanee, GA?
At Sugarloaf Signature Dentistry, professional teeth whitening typically ranges from $199 to $399, depending on whether you choose in-office whitening or custom take-home trays. We always provide a detailed estimate during your consultation so you know exactly what to expect before starting treatment.
How long does the teeth whitening process take, and how many visits are needed?
Our in-office whitening treatment usually takes about 60 to 90 minutes and can often be completed in a single visit. If you opt for our custom take-home trays, you'll wear them daily for about 10–14 days to achieve optimal results. We’ll review your schedule and goals to recommend the best option during your appointment.
Are your dentists licensed and experienced with cosmetic teeth whitening?
Yes, all dentists at Sugarloaf Signature Dentistry are fully licensed in Georgia and have specialized experience in cosmetic dentistry, including teeth whitening. Our team regularly completes advanced training to stay current with the latest whitening technologies and techniques.
What can I expect during my teeth whitening appointment?
At your appointment, we’ll first evaluate your dental health and discuss your whitening goals. For in-office whitening, we’ll protect your gums, apply a professional-grade whitening gel, and use a specialized light to activate the gel. The process is comfortable and you’ll see results immediately. If you choose take-home trays, we’ll take impressions of your teeth and provide you with custom-fitted trays and instructions.
Do you offer any guarantees or touch-ups if I’m not satisfied with my whitening results?
We want you to be thrilled with your smile! While individual results vary depending on your starting shade and habits, we stand by our work. If you don’t see a noticeable improvement, please contact us within two weeks of your treatment. We offer complimentary touch-ups or adjustments within that period to ensure you’re satisfied.
